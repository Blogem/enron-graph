# Manual Verification Tests for Success Criteria SC-002, SC-004, SC-005

These success criteria require manual verification through the Graph Explorer UI.

## SC-002: Navigate 100-500 Nodes Smoothly

**Requirement**: Users can successfully navigate and explore a graph subset of 100-500 nodes with smooth pan/zoom interactions (no lag or stuttering)

### Test Steps:
1. Start the Graph Explorer: `cd cmd/explorer && wails dev`
2. Wait for the app to load 100 initial nodes
3. Perform the following interactions and verify smooth performance:
   - Pan the graph in all directions (drag with mouse)
   - Zoom in and out (mouse wheel or pinch)
   - Click and drag nodes to reposition them
   - Click on the "Load Random Nodes" if needed to load more

### Pass Criteria:
- [ ] All pan operations are smooth with no stuttering
- [ ] Zoom operations respond immediately (<100ms perceived delay)
- [ ] Graph rendering remains at 30+ FPS during interactions
- [ ] No lag when clicking/dragging nodes
